## 🧪 Active Research

<details open>
<summary><b>🧠  Mamba & State Space Models</b></summary>

> Exploring **VMamba**, **Spatial-Mamba**, and **hybrid CNN–SSM** architectures for remote sensing segmentation and change detection. Emphasis on computational efficiency and long-range spatial reasoning without the quadratic cost of full self-attention.

**Key topics:** VMamba · PlainMamba · Spatial-Mamba · Selective Scan · SSM efficiency

</details>

<details open>
<summary><b>🛰️ Remote Sensing Change Detection</b></summary>

> Researching dual-stream and bi-temporal architectures using **wavelet-domain fusion**, **edge-aware losses**, and **uncertainty estimation** for robust urban change detection in satellite imagery.

**Key topics:** BIT · ChangeFormer · SNUNet · Wavelet decomposition · Uncertainty maps

</details>

<details open>
<summary><b>🧍 Group Activity Recognition (GAR)</b></summary>

> Investigating spatiotemporal learning strategies that integrate **physical constraints** (motion consistency, feasibility bounds) into deep activity recognition models. Targeting physically grounded inference rather than purely statistical pattern matching.

**Key topics:** COMPOSER · AT · GroupFormer · Spatiotemporal graphs · Physical plausibility

</details>
