## 📄 Published Research

### 🛰️ IEEE IGARSS 2026 — *Accepted*

**A Controlled Benchmark of Visual State-Space Backbones with Domain-Shift and Boundary Analysis for Remote-Sensing Segmentation**

> Five backbone families — VMamba, MambaVision, Spatial-Mamba, DeepLabv3+ (CNN), and UNetFormer (Transformer) — evaluated under one pipeline, one lightweight U-Net decoder, and one loss function (TriBraid: Lovász + Focal + Boundary). Fair apples-to-apples comparison across SSM, CNN, and Transformer paradigms on LoveDA and ISPRS Potsdam benchmarks.
